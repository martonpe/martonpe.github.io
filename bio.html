Peter B Marton

BIO

Peter B Marton is a computer scientitst and multidisciplinary artist whose omnivorous practice spans computer generated imagery, video, music and performance. Oscillating between personal excavation and world-making, his work asks, with both sincerity and subversion how we might - collectively and individually - save ourselves from ourselves.

Through a practice that fuses visual poetics with conceptual rigor, this relentless introspection expands outward, evolving into a larger project of cultural reckoning as Marton plots against the status quo, wielding art as both weapon and lifeline.
